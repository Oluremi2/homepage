Top three places to visit in Ibadan

Enjoy the view from the bowers tower

Bowers tower is a historical landmark in Ibadan. It is a 60-foot tower that was built in 1936 in honour of Captain Robert Lister Bower - the first british resident in Nigeria.

Enjoy the sweet smell of fresh leafs and trees at the Agodi Gardens

This green oasis of peace in Ibadan draws locals and travelers to its many attractions.Here you can spot turtles ,monkeys and peacocks at the agodi Zoo.

Go for a historcal moment at Ado-Awaye

Ado-Awaye is a town in Oyo State Nigeria. It is popular for its hill(Oke Ado),upon which is a lake(Iyalake),one of only tow suspended lakes in world.
